What is Bitcoin?

Bitcoin is a decentralized cryptocurrency meaning that it operates without the supervision of a sole administrator. As the first decentralized cryoptocurrency, it has gathered increasingly more widespread recogniton and adoption. In fact, El Salvador's government has legalized it's use as legal tender apart from the United States Dollar.

The currency is divided into "bitcoins" in which the lowest possible units are denominated as "satoshis", in recognition of the asset's unknown creator or creators' likely pseudonym, Satoshi Nakamoto.
